School Library

Was built in 1980

The mission of school libraries is to ensure that students and staff are able to use ideas and information. The libraries provide access to materials in all formats. They help increase students' interest in reading, viewing, and using information and ideas.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

An auditorium is the right place for any social, cultural, and educational activity in a school. An auditorium is also known as an assembly hall. It is an essential part of a school as it has multiple uses to organize celebrations and recreational activities.
